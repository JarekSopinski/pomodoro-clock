Help

A typical Pomodoro consists of 25 minutes of highly focused working or learning session, followed by 5 minutes of short break. After 4 sessions you should take a longer break, usually about 15 minutes.
 
Click on a timer to start counting time. Click again to pause.
 
You can change the length of each part, but only when timer is paused. Keep in mind that this will reset current session.
 
You can also use reset button to restore initial settings and restart whole cycle while still keeping track of completed Pomodoros.
